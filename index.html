The Magick Manuscript is a living Notion database built for modern practitioners who want reliable, searchable magickal knowledge in one place. It brings correspondences, rituals, entities, plants, symbols, timing, and more into one cross-linked system — so you can study deeply and apply what you learn in practice.

This is the grimoire I always wished existed. So I built it.

behind the manuscript

What began as a
personal grimoire

I'm autistic, and this work became a deep special interest. What started as my own private notes spiralled into something far larger — a meticulously researched, cross-referenced database that reflects the relationships between every aspect of magickal practice.

Every entry is drawn from reputable published sources: books by established scholars and practitioners in witchcraft, folklore, and occult studies. The depth of traditional research, made searchable for a modern practice.

My husband handles the technical architecture. I handle the research. Together we built the resource we couldn't find anywhere else.

my calling

Built from
lived practice

My personal practice is rooted in plant work, herbs, candles, tarot, runes, and crystals — with a strong focus on protection magick. Every entry in the Manuscript reflects something I have personally researched and, in most cases, used.

This is not a curated aesthetic or a content play. It is a genuine attempt to preserve the depth and reliability of traditional occult scholarship in a format that actually serves a modern practice.
